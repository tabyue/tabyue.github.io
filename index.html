What is Embodied AI?

Embodied AI (Embodied Intelligence) is an intelligent system based on physical perception and action, achieving information acquisition, decision-making, and execution through interaction with the environment. Unlike traditional AI that "thinks in computers," it emphasizes "learning by entering the physical world."

As a foundation connecting cyberspace with physical-world applications (such as intelligent mechatronic systems and smart manufacturing), Embodied AI is a key technical pathway toward Artificial General Intelligence (AGI).
